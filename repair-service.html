About Warranty

Miele and SOBO provide a 7 year warranty on the motors, product casings and 5 year warranty on labor and parts for most of the products. And we are the authorized repair center for Miele and Sebo in North Vancouver. If there is a malfunction with your vacuum, don’t worry, you’re covered.

note: Miele will not cover the vacuum under the warranty if you’ve taken the machine to a non-authorized repair center.
